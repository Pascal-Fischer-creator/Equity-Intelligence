How it works
For each stock that appears in ≥2 snapshots, the engine computes per-metric deltas. It then correlates each metric's change with the resulting composite-score change across all stocks to identify leading indicators. Stocks whose most recent metric movements align with those leading indicators receive higher BUY signals. Confidence blends pattern strength × data coverage × signal magnitude. This is a heuristic backtest on your own data — not investment advice.
